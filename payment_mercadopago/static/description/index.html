Mercado Pago payment

Mercadopago is the largest online payment platform in Latam.

Features

This module integrates a checkout form to make payments. Supports automated payments without CVV for subscriptions and card authorizations.
  • Online payment with embedded credit card form (single environment)
  • Payment Status Tracking
  • Recurring payments (Subscriptions)
  • Save Cards

Inline Forms: Customer pays without leaving odoo.

One module for all. Supports Website, Subscriptions, Payment Link, Portal and backend flows.

Support for tokenization Allows saving tokens or registering cards for later payments.
